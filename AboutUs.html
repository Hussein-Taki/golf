Balmoral Club

Our Story

Established in the scenic highlands of Balmoral in 1812, our company has been synonymous with prestige and class for over a century. Our rich history is steeped in tradition and excellence, reflecting the timeless values of our Scottish heritage. We are proud of our roots and committed to maintaining the high standards set by our founders.



Our dedication to quality and craftsmanship has earned us a reputation as a beacon of luxury and refinement. As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our commitment to carry forward this legacy of excellence and sophistication.

Why Us?

At Balmoral Club, we offer more than just a game of golf; we provide a legacy of excellence. For those who demand the finest experiences in life, our club is a sanctuary of sophistication and a bastion of bespoke service.

Why Golf?

The natural beauty of our surroundings, coupled with the sport’s inherent elegance and precision, resonated deeply with our founders. They saw in golf a reflection of the values we hold dear: tradition, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Our foray into golf began with crafting bespoke golf equipment, each piece a testament to our commitment to quality and craftsmanship. Over the years, we have honed our skills, constantly innovating while staying true to our heritage. Today, we are proud to be a part of the prestigious golfing community, contributing to the sport’s rich history and tradition with our high-class golfing products.
